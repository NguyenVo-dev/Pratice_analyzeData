The driving in AI is a great sign for the cybersecurity sector. However, these day AI seems to be a bubble that the hype about it is starting to cool down.The growth of AI is quite slow lately. People are more concerned about their privacy while using AI. In fact, many new AI is created so that it can run locally on individual device.

The trend of AI that it is starting to shift to offline version is a sign people care more about cybersecurity. The price of HACK is slightly smaller than the current price of TCHEY. Its dividend is also not so appealing that it is only arounf 0.12%

Still, HACK need to be watched closely. It is not a safe investment as it growth seems to be depent on the hype of AI. If at the end, AI is a bubble, then HACK will have a long fall. It will not the end of HACK through, cybersecurity already a sector that is worth to notice before AI. As long as the internet still exist, cybersecurity will survive

There is a trend in storing data that data will be stored in cloud. Storing data in cloud is not a new thing. However, it is only paid attention these few year. If in the future that all data will be store in the cloud, people will start to cocern about how they can keep their data secure
